BODY MASS INDEX (BMI) CALCULATOR

The Body Mass Index or BMI, measures the human body shape based on your weight (kg) and height (cm).


Features:

  • Calculate your BMI


    • How to Use:

    1. Enter your weight, in Kilograms.
    2. Enter your height, in Centimeters.


    BMI Category:

  • Less than 18.4 – Underweight
  • From 18.5.0 to 24.9 – Normal weight
  • From 25 to 29.9 – Overweight
  • From 30 to 34.9 – Moderately obese
  • From 35 to 39.9 – Severely obese
  • More than 40 – Very Severely obese
